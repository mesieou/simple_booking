Frequently asked questions

Individual Tax returns

How long does the process take?

Processing time is 48 business hours. 48 hours after you send us your information, we will send you your declaration ready to be electronically signed or we will contact you to request additional information.

How much does the service cost?

The base price is $99.
If you need help applying for a medicare exemption certificate, an extra $35 applies.

Why should I use Y&S accounting Brisbane's online tax return service?

1. For the confidence that your tax return will be prepared by a professional tax return preparer authorised by the Australian tax office.
2. We specialise in international students and temporary residents in Australia.
3. You can complete the whole process from the comfort of your own home.

If I don't have a bank account in Australia, can I receive my refund in someone else's account or in a bank account in another country?

Yes, we can arrange for you to receive your refund in a third party bank account or a bank account in another country. A trust account fee of $55 applies.

What is a medicare exemption certificate and do I need one?

People who are not entitled to Medicare can apply for an exemption from the Medicare levy on their tax return. To obtain an exemption, you must not be entitled to Medicare and you must apply for Medicare levy exemption certification with the Services Australia department.
